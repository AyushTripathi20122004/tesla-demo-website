Building a Sustainable, More Resilient Grid

The future of renewable energy relies on large-scale industrial energy storage. Megapack is a powerful, integrated battery system that provides clean, reliable, cost-effective energy storage to help stabilize the grid and prevent outages. Reducing our reliance on fossil fuels and strengthening our grid infrastructure will make sustainable energy more accessible and affordable for everyone on Earth. Learn more in our Master Plan Part 3.

Transforming Industrial Energy Storage

Megapack is changing the way the grid is powered. Operating in over 65 countries, Megapack provides critical grid support globally and can support multi-GWh projects.

Easy to Install

Each unit ships fully assembled with a built-in foundation and integrated wireways, reducing installation costs by 25%.2 Its flexible configuration allows for faster installation, and over 99% of deployments are delivered on time. We also offer support during installation and throughout operation.

Safe to Operate

Megapack offers reliable and safe energy storage. It is designed as a single, vertically integrated system with hardware and controls that reduce fire risk. It meets over 40 global safety requirements, ensuring a safe environment for site workers and local communities.

Built for Performance

Megapack comes with a 20-year warranty and performance guarantees, ensuring operational capacity throughout the lifetime of the system. Each installation receives 24/7 support and automated diagnostics, with an average uptime of over 99%, even in harsh environments.

Power More

Megapack installations are fully customizable and can be deployed at scale, making them suitable for a variety of project sizes, locations and applications.

Standalone Utilities

Stabilizes grid voltage, frequency and capacity levels, replacing fossil-fuel peaker plants and keeping the grid running smoothly.

Renewable Paired

Maintains the stability and reliability of renewable energy sources on the grid by storing and discharging energy depending on the availability of solar and wind energy.

Commercial and Industrial

Offers commercial businesses more reliable energy access and allows them to share excess energy with the grid during peak demand hours.

Microgrids

Allows electricity to be pooled and then distributed or managed without a connection to the grid.
